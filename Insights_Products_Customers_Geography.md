## SUMMARY: KEY INSIGHTS

### Product Insights
✅ T-Shirts & Shirts dominate (77% revenue) - focus but diversify  
✅ Size M, L, XL are core (80% orders) - maintain stock  
✅ Plus-size growing (13% orders) - increase allocation to 20%  
✅ Premium items (Perfume, Blazer) have margin potential  
✅ Low performers (Watch, Shoes) need repositioning or exit  

### Customer Insights
✅ High-value customers (39% of base, 60% revenue) - retention critical  
✅ Medium-value customers (38% of base) - upgrade opportunity  
✅ Cancellations (7.5%) - major satisfaction issue  
✅ Return rate (6.8%) - quality/expectation gap  
✅ B2B underdeveloped (0.7%) - massive growth potential  

### Geographic Insights
✅ Metro-concentrated (60% from 5 states) - diversification needed  
✅ Top 7 cities drive 40% revenue - focus but expand  
✅ Tier-2 cities emerging - growth opportunity  
✅ Regional preferences vary - customize offerings  
✅ Southern states prefer formal wear, Northern casual
